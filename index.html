Nationwide Resource for Premium “Turn Key” Window, Door, Glass product, and all Fenestration Installation services from conception to application to maintenance.

Replacing windows and doors in luxury historical homes is a large part of our expertise. The use of the patented YAK, our professional grade construction products, and our combined knowledge of the industry allow our team to work with ease and precision on any project.

Sequel’s specialty is the precise, surgical removal of existing windows that allows for the new windows to be installed with competence and with minimal disruptions all while preserving the interior and exterior of the home.

At Sequel, we are very proud to be affiliated with top manufacturers. Our window and door installation company consists of a staff of experienced and certified installers who are knowledgeable and on the forefront of new technology for window and door installation. In addition, Sequel also has a sales and marketing team who are involved in the project from start to finish to ensure customer needs are met. The team at Sequel works to create a smooth and flawless transition for each phase of the project.

Having your windows and doors installed properly is of the utmost importance to the manufacturer, builder, and the end user.
